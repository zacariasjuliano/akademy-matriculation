## Akademy Matriculation Module

The **Akademy Matriculation (Enrollment Management)** module is a complementary module responsible for **managing school enrollments**, keeping all information organized and accessible to the institution.

It offers advanced tools for **enrollments, applications, and transfers**, allowing for centralized and efficient monitoring of each student's performance and progress.

### ğŸ¯ Purpose

This module centralizes the **student enrollment lifecycle**, including:
- Registration and management of candidates,
- Submission and tracking of applications,
- Evaluation of applications according to criteria,
- Management of student transfers,
- Association of students to courses and disciplines,
- Generation of official enrollment and transfer reports.

It is designed for use by **schools, training centers, universities, and educational authorities**.
